    • I am an artist, programmer, composer, and educator.


    As a student at New York University studying Music composition, I started with a focus on writing electronic music and became increasingly interested in writing and coding “computer music” in the dataflow programming language Max/Msp.

    I furthered these skills in an intensive course at IRCAM in Paris and while working at Harvestworks Media Arts Center where I coded for, built, and maintained a space for multi-speaker compositions to be experienced.

    I continued taking courses within the Music Technology program as well as courses in the Computer Science department at NYU, where I received a minor in Web Programming and Applications. During my senior year, I worked as a Programming Tutor for the Music Technology department, as well as a Studio Technician.

    As a composer and performer, audience interaction and experience are everything to me. As a Web Programmer, I still hold that concept at the core of my work. I strive to craft both web projects and compositions that get people engaged and leave a lasting impression.

    I’m currently looking for positions in Digital Marketing and front-end development.

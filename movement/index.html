    • "movement" is an installation-inspired web piece that explores the Web Audio API and p5js.

    It is a reimagining of William Kolomyjec's 1977 'Random Squares.'
    As you move your cursor from the left to the right, more nested sqaures are generated, and more vocal tracks are put into the mix.
    up and down movement controls framerate as well as amplitude modulation of all of the vocals.

    Then feel free to explore this instrument.

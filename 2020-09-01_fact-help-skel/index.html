Basic Tech Requirements

If you can access the web, you can participate in the FACT Inspection And Accreditation Workshop! However, to be successful we strongly encourage using a PC or Mac with a keyboard, camera, microphone, and strong internet connection.
In general, here are the specific requirements:

  • PC, Mac, tablet, or smartphone (although we recommend using a PC or Mac)
  • Internet connection (wired is better than wi-fi)
  • Modern web browser. The FACT Inspection And Accreditation Workshop will support the latest versions of Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not fully supported and you should use a different browser.
  • Please upgrade to the latest version of your chosen web browser. Although our system supports several versions of Chrome and Firefox, we do not support older versions of Edge or Internet Explorer.
  • If using Microsoft Edge, please make sure you have the latest version. You can download it(the latest version of Microsoft Edge) here.
  • Not sure if your browser is up-to-date? Check if it is here
  • An external or integrated microphone (external is recommended) for meeting sessions. We recommend using a headset with built-in microphone.
  • If you plan to participate in the FACT Inspection And Accreditation Workshop from a tablet or smartphone, know that some features will be harder to access, although watching sessions will work just fine.

Logging In

  • Please log in before September 21st so that you can check your credentials and check your tech! You don’t want to miss even a minute of the FACT Inspection And Accreditation Workshop!
  • After going to the gate page and clicking "Enter," you will be directed to log into MatrixMaxx. Your MatrixMaxx account must be registered for this event.
  • After you log-in once, you will not be asked for log in again. If, by chance, you do get logged out, or if you want to access the conference from a different device or browser, you will need to repeat the steps to log-in.

Accessing the FACT sessions and Meeting Rooms

There’s a lot of tech going on at the Conference. Please make sure you can access the events and presentations by clicking this link to visit the Information Desk. While there:

  • Make sure you can view the videos.
  • Make sure you have given your browser permission to use your speaker and microphone for the meeting.
  • Make sure you can use the Attendee Chat so that you can post comments and questions for the presenters or to message other Attendees.

Attendee Search and Updating Your Profile

During the FACT Inspection And Accreditation Workshop, you can network with your fellow attendees! The Attendee landing page will show you a random collection of attendees. You can click on a person’s name to get more information or click Send a Message to send that a person a message. All messages will stay within this conference, i.e., they don’t trigger emails.

Want to get more out of the conference? Update your profile! You can upload, enter contact information, even display your interests!

Accessing the Schedule

We’ve got a packed, 2-day schedule. Preview the schedule by going to the the Schedule page.

  • To view each day’s schedule, hover your mouse over the bar of dates and scroll up or down to navigate through them. Click one to view upcoming events for that day.
  • To view the recordings of presentations after they have aired, please click on Previously Recorded.
